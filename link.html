Coffee

coffee, beverage brewed from the roasted and ground seeds of the tropical evergreen coffee plants of African origin.

Coffee is one of the three most popular beverages in the world (alongside water and tea) and one of the most profitable international commodities. Coffee is a beloved beverage known for its ability to fine-tune your focus and boost your energy levels. In fact, many people depend on their daily cup of joe right when they wake up to get their day started on the right foot.
